a. path
	smallest: any path with one edge(eg. a-b, g-m)
	largest: some path including all nodes (eg. m-g-h-k-i-j-a-b-c-d-e-f)
b. cycle
	smallest: i-j-k-j
	largest: g-h-k-i-j-a-b-c-d-e-f-g
c. spanning tree(including all the vertices of graph)
	smallest: m-g-h-k-i-j-a-b-c-d-e-f
	largest: m-g-h-k-i-j-a-b-c-d-e-f
d. vertex degree
	smallest: 1
	largest: 5 f b
e. clique(subset of all vertices of graph form a complete graph)
	smallest: one node!
	largest: b,c,d,e,f 5
